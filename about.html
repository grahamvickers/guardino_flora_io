About Us

For over 5 years, FLORA.IO has been the leader in environment plant enhancing equipement.

FLORA.IO was established as a Canadian family-owned business in 2016. Through innovation, research, and real-life experience, we provide solutions that will help your plants to grow and strive. We develop affordable solutions for large and small scale operations and we take pride in our designs and equipment.

Our Vision

Our vision is Helping Growers Worldwide.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to supply farmers, big and small, with high-quality products, support and technology.

FLORA.IO is invested in our customers’ success. We work with anyone interested in growing their plants efficiently, whether you’re a hobby grower, middle school student or business owner.
